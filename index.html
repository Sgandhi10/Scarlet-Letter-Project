About Us

Our Mission

Our mission is to help educate students and families about public shaming. Public shaming can have crippling effects on victims, so we want to start a movement to end it.

Our Plan

We plan to end public shaming by educating students and families about it through videos. Our videos will educate students and families about its negative effects, and encourage victims to speak about how it affected them.

Our Vision

We invision a world where public-shaming is a thing of the past. We want to help spread the word to families and other schools to help encourage a better society for our children and families to grow up in.

Call To Action

Join the cause and help us spread the word to other schools and students to help stop this epidemic from spreading.
